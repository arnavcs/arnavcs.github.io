My 2025 Resolution

Jan 01 2025

As we go into the new year of 2025, I would like to document my new years resolution. I have decided to complete at least one book every month of the year of 2025.

In the year of 2024, I did not dedicate nearly as much time as I had wanted into reading, but at the end of the year, I finished Braiding Sweetgrass by Robin Wall Kimmerer which had a great impact on me, and really got me excited about reading more again. To keep track, I will be adding a section to my webpage for rating the books that I read.
